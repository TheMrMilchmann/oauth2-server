Data

We keep access and error logs of this website. Your IP-Address may appear in these logs, however, we never use the IP-Address to perform any kind of tracking or analytics
If you register on this website using the "GW2Auth-Login", your login information is managed by the AWS-Service "Amazon Cognito". We can't see your password used to register or login using the "GW2Auth-Login" at any time. If you wish to have your "GW2Auth-Login" to be deleted, please contact us via E-Mail.
The login of this website is realized by using different OAuth2-Providers. For any login provider, we only store the most minimal information possible to identify your account. That is: a combination of the chosen login provider and your unique User-ID given to us by the login provider.
We store data that has been explicitly created by you using your Account-Management.
In the nature of this website as a OAuth2-Authorization-Server for other applications, we share a limited amount of data with external applications that have been authorized by you. If you login on an external application using GW2Auth and authorize it to access your GW2-Accounts, we pass the following information to the external website in order to give them the authorized access:
A unique User-ID that identifies your GW2Auth-Account at the external application. The shared User-ID is unique per external application.
The unique GW2-Account-IDs you authorized the external application to have access to
GW2-API-Subtokens for the GW2-Accounts you authorized the external application to have access to.
External applications receive OAuth2 Access-Tokens and Refresh-Tokens once your authorized the application access
